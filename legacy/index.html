A Visionary Builder of Philippine Real Estate

Anthony "Tonton" Leuterio is a Filipino entrepreneur from Cebu City, recognized across the industry for building one of the largest real estate marketing networks in the Philippines and pioneering digital property marketing at scale.

Founder of Filipino Homes CEO – Leuterio Realty Motivational Speaker Real Estate Influencer Marketing Consultant Licensed Realtor Property Investor

Beginning his real estate journey in 2008, Anthony built a nationwide system empowering agents, brokers, and sellers to list and market properties through cutting-edge online platforms.
